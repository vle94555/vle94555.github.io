The Band LANY

Some information on the band LANY, as well as my experience seeing them in concert!

LANY is a band of three members, and they have concerts all over the world. Some examples are Japan, Korea, and the Philippines. Their genre of music is Indie pop, Synth-pop, and Dream pop. Their tickets are usually around $30 for general admission. My brother and I have been to one of their concerts, and it was incredible. It was a lot of fun. There weren't too many people there. It was a nice amount of people. My brother was at the front of the venue, near the stage. I was in the back, but it was still a lot of fun. There was a lot of jumping and singing. The band had cool and colorful background screens for the crowd to look at as well. After the concert, my brother and I actually waited to see if we could meet the members. It turned out that they were too tired to come and meet everyone, so we ended up leaving. Overall, it was a great experience though.
